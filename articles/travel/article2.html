TOP 2 Hiking experience

Khalilah Sutton 6:49 AM Technology

Based off of the local adventurer blog one trail you should go on is the Inca Trail. The Inca Trail is 26 miles. Located in the Andes Mountains. It has ruins, jungle, sand, and amazing mountain scenery.

It’s not an easy terrain to tackle but it has many ruins to entertain making the trip worthwhile. The Incas had two main uses of transportation on the roads. They used the chasqui (runners) for relaying messages throughout the empire and llamas & alpacas for transporting goods. The chasqui were known as the runners of the empire.

Another trail that’s exciting and full of experience would be the Kilimanjaro in Tanzania, Africa. The terrain is 19,340 feet. It features a large stratovolcano which is composed of three smaller one. Mawenzi and Shira. The night time temperatures can range between 20 and -20 degrees, so make sure you wear or bring some type of coat or jackets. Due to Mount Kilimanjaro's height, the mountain likes createing its own weather. Wind will be generally light. Having certain clothes(small jackets) would be most important.
