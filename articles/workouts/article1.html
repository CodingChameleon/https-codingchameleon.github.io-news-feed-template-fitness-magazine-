Stamina and Cardio Workouts

Zoe Valladares 6:49 AM Workouts

Diets? Lifestyles? Giving up? Don’t! To increase your stamina and burn that reappearing fat, follow these Cardio Workout tips and you will love the result!

  • TIP 1:
  • Intervals to increase stamina
    To increase your stamina sprinting in intervals and increasing the distance every time you work out is one of the best ways. Make sure you stretch before you run because you don’t want to pull you hamstrings or damage your quadriceps. According to The 8 Best Stretches to Do Before Running (2017) doing dynamic stretches before a warm-up run (1-3 miles) is the best to stretch your joints instead of static stretches. Doing these intervals are fine on any type of terrain, but making sure that you are nutritionally capable, hydrated, and well stretched for the warm-up or workout is necessary stated The 8 Best Stretches to Do Before Running (2017).

  • TIP 2:

    • Push yourself every time you work out to increase your reps or time on the treadmill.

    Treadmill workouts can be more than just running. The amount of workouts you can do on a treadmill can be a beneficial cardio workout and help with your stamina. According to Tricks of the Tread: Treadmill Interval Workouts (2015) there is strength training workouts that can be done of treadmills and it consists of increasing the workout intensity.


    For example:
  • Start with a walk
  • Continue with a jog
  • Run at a natural speed
  • Reduce the speed
  • Walk
  • Jog
  • Run at natural speed
  • Walk
  • Sprint
  • REPEAT

  • TIP 3:

    • Don’t eat a lot of fats but increase carbs before workouts to gain energy
    When you are ready to do an intense workout that includes a lot of cardio you want to make sure that you have a lot of energy. The best way to get this energy is to eat carbohydrates.
    But not any type of carbs, super carbs.
    Yes, you may want to gorge on pasta, and devour pizza, however, bananas, potatoes, pasta, nuts, and oatmeal and healthier sources of carbs are your best bet. In the article, The Right Way to Carbo-load Before a Race (2017) depending on your weight, the intensity of the workout, and the amount of calories you consume you should find the right amount of carbs to eat and eat them 2-4 before the workout. If you are doing a marathon, dinner would be the best time to stock up on carbs.



  • TIP 4:

    • RUN FOREST RUN

    Running is one of the best exercises for cardio and stamina alike. It is cardio and if you push yourself you will be able to increase your stamina. Running everyday, the same distance will increase your stamina. Once you lower the time you spend running that distance, increase it and continue doing that.



  • TIP 5:
    • Don’t look at the scale

    You will be tempted to look at your scale to make sure that the work you are putting in is worth it. Although it is not always about your weight, you may gain weight due to the amount of muscle you build. If you are tempted to look at a scale, invest in scales that don’t just calculate your weight. Tips and tricks to burn fat and increase stamina. To recap, push yourself and don’t give up when you feel a bit tired, that always can lead to a decrease in stamina. Cut fats increase carbs before high intensity workouts -- it will let you be the best you. Running can be a great way to increase your stamina, especially if you lift more weights then do your cardio. Lastly, the scale, don’t look at it. You may gain weight, it doesn’t mean it's fat weight though, it is most likely muscle mass and water. Your fat percentage will go down if these steps are followed.
