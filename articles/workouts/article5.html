At Home workouts

ZValladares 1:11 PM Workouts

Can’t afford the long term payments with a gym?

Make your home a gym!

This will show you different home gym ideas that will change your life and help you reach your fitness goal.

Pull Up Bar

    SUPPLIES NEEDED:


  • A doorway
  • 5 pieces of metal piping
  • Metal faceplates
  • Drill
  • Screws
  • Four 3 socket tees

Instructions:

According to 10 Homemade Gym Equipment Ideas to Build Your Own Gym there is an ability to create a standing pipe pull up bar. Although this would be much more expensive to make and much more space consuming. According to DIY Free Standing Pull-Up Bar the materials needed to create the pull up bar would be approximately $500, when buying one could be less than $60.

Decreasing the amount of piping a materials needed to create this doorway pull up bar will decrease the price to about $60.

To start, what you will need is a doorway. If there is a door that is ok. The bar will protrude enough from the doorway that it will not be a problem.

Connect the face plate above the door with screws and the drill. Attach the pipes to the faceplate. Then attach the 3 socket tees for both pipes, and connect another pair of pipes to the top of each Tee. Then add the second set of tees and add another faceplate pair. There you go, a pull up bar!

Dip Station


    SUPPLIES NEEDED

  • 2x4s
  • Piping
  • Drill
  • screws

Instructions:



According to 10 Homemade Gym Equipment Ideas to Build Your Own Gym a dip station is a great ab and tricep workout. It also strengthens the back. Using 2x4s in a large space and creating a box frame. Then in the middle of the box frame have 2 pairs of horizontal supports. One pair being 2x4s and the other being the metal piping as shown in the image.



Squat Rack

    SUPPLIES NEEDED

  • Pipes
  • Elbows
  • 3 tees
  • More Pipe connections


Instructions:



According to Home there are many ways to create a squat rack, although making it out of metal is the most safe. On Home there are instructions to make a squat rack. The rack will take a large amount of space but it is well worth it. You have a body building station in your garage!



Monkey Bars



    SUPPLIES NEEDED

  • Metal Piping
  • Metal Connectors

Instructions:



Monkey bars aren't just for kids. They can be used to build strength in your upper body and core. In addition, they can help improve coordination. If you do any type of Parkour or Ninja training, monkey bars are a great training tool. According to 10 Homemade Gym Equipment Ideas to Build Your Own Gym you will need some space for this metal jungle. It will be on the ceiling, upside down, and completely accessible.



These tools, upfront, are more expensive than most gym memberships, although they are not more expensive than the long term payments with a gym. These will help you reach your fitness goals and help you save money in the long run.
