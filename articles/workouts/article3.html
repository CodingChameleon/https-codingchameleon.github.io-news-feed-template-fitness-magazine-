Muscle Gain

Zoe Valladares 6:49 AM Workouts

So you’ve lost a significant amount of body fat, you are underweight, or you just really want to get ripped, follow these steps! Once you follow these tips you will have all the information you need to get to your goal of muscle gain.



Workouts:

  • Lifting large amounts of weight
    • This goes hand in hand with sleep
    • If you lift heavy weights you need to recover, this will allow for maximum weight lifting.
  • Cardio
    • Muscle gain is not only about lifting heavy weights. Cardio, especially high reps of intense targeted workouts will benefit muscle growth.
    • If bodybuilding is a goal you are striving for, looking towards cardio is not the best bet. Although it will get rid of any excess unhealthy fat that is lingering.

Mentality

  • SLEEEEEP
    • Sleep will help you grow, rebuild, and strengthen your body. Without putting your body to rest for 8 plus hours you are damaging your body. This goes hand in hand with sleep
  • Set goals
    • If you don’t set goals to where you want to be, document your changes, or put in any effort to caring about what you want to do, then it is completely useless to put your body through something without an end goal.
    • Some ways to set goals are:
      • Calculate the amount of days you are working out.
      • Create an excel sheet that keeps track of measurements, weight, BMI, and muscle mass breakdown.
      • Make vlogs of you working out so you can visually see progress.

Nutrition

  • Eat
    • Large amounts of protein, calcium and carbs will help you with your goal. Eating foods that superfoods that will not add to your unhealthy fat consumption will overall help you gain muscle mass.
    • Take Supplements
      • This is only if you are not getting the amount of super nutrients that you need from your meals. THIS IS NOT A REPLACEMENT! Eat good foods that are natural, although if an extra boost is needed take supplements.

Types of Workouts

  • Since these workouts will be intense you will work certain parts of your body.
  • Creating 4 types of workout plans for different days will allow for premium recovery time.
  • Working out vigorously for 4 days a week and giving yourself 3 rest days gives the best ratio for muscle gain and muscle recovery.
  • Example:
    • Upper Body Plan 1
    • Lower Body Plan 1
    • Rest
    • Upper Body Plan 2 (to rest other upper body parts from Plan 1)
    • Lower Body Plan 2 (to rest other lower body parts from Plan 1)
    • Rest
    • Rest
  • These workouts can consist of:
  • High reps of heavy weight lifting
  • Cardio workouts (not always running)
    • Can be targeted cardio workouts, for example -- walking lunges
  • Thigh targeted workouts
  • Full body workouts
  • Chest and tricep and bicep targeted workouts
  • AND MORE
  • THESE WORKOUTS ARE ALL HIGH REP LOW SETS (EX. 3 sets of 10)


These tips will help you gain muscle. Creating these goals for yourself and following these tips will really help you.
