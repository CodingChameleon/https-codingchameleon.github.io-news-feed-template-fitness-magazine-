Video Games = Fitness + Health

Naia McNatt 7:11 AM Technology

People are always on the lookout to find the next big thing to help with their fitness. The main attraction in video games are that they provide exercises that are fun yet productive. If you are looking for the answer look no further for video games are only a console or VR headset away. Right now, you might be asking to yourself, “Why video games?” Here 4 reasons as to why video games are actually proven to help, sometimes even better than regular exercising!:

  • (Makes exercising more fun and exciting) According to Virtuagym, “ … video games … has the possibility of flipping the idea ‘exercise isn’t fun’ on its head.”
  • (Actual Proof of videogames working) According to software tester Juan Espinosa, “The Oculus Rift was something I had always wanted to get … I decided to use it for fitness … When I first started out, I weighed about 190 … I weigh 172 pounds.”
  • (More motivating than regular exercising) Due to the fact that in games you are usually working towards an in game goal you are more motivated cause you can visually see the challenge
  • (Easier to access than a gym so you will exercise more) Because a video game stays within your home and doesn’t have a closing time it is always accessible and which makes you wanna exercise more because you don’t have an excuse to not do it.

In conclusion these 4 reasons show why video games help out with fitness which in turn helps you maintain good health.
