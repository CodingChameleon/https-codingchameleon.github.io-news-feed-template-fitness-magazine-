Swim Gear Look Here:

Naia McNatt 6:66 AM Technology

Finis technology will help swimmers all over reach their Olympic potential. With the groundbreaking technology of Finis Duo, you can use vibrations to listen to your favorite music to block out surrounding sounds as you practice hard. With the Swimsense Live accompanying the Finis Duo you can optimize your time in the water with real time and accurate recording of your swim workout data such as your stroke count, total distance, time/pace and more.

I would recommend the Finis Duo. What other headphones do you know that won’t break after extended time under water, has thought of this type of vibration technology, and can help you reach your next level as you mellow out with your music during training.

Finis Duo is,” Tested to perform at a depth of 3 meters (10 feet) for 30 minutes.”

This proves that they are not only for regular competitive swimmers but would also work for high performing divers or people just looking to set the next Guiness World Record for holding their breath.

The Finis Duo has, “Up to 7 hours of battery life”

This means that you can practice your break strokes and backstrokes for a quarter of the day before having to plug up.

There are many perks to the Finis Swimsense Live. First of all, it has a enough storage space for all of the 14 workouts that you that you will do

The Finis Swimsense Live also, “ … includes magnetic dock with USB cable for charging.”
The Finis Swimsense also has a scoring system called “ … Swolf … “ for those competitive swimmers who wish to compete against their friends.

Best of all there is no time limit to the amount of time the Finis Swimsense Live can spend underwater so you can practice your backstroke and the watch is constantly smacking the water. This shows the many advantages that the Finis Swimsense Live will have to your swimming career.

In conclusion, both the Finis Swimsense and the Finis Duo help you improve your swimming game through the various features.
