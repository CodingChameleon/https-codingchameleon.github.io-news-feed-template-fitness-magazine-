Healthy Snacks On The Go

Yessenia 1:54 PM Nutrition

Imagine that...

...you just finished a nice meal but a few hours go by but you start to feel hungry gain. At this point you’ll mostly likely choose to have a snack however how do you narrow down a better option when it comes to snacking. For the record snacking is alright as long is it done smartly and with moderations.

Hydration or Hunger?

One thing to keep in mind if you believe that you are hungry is to check if you’ve have drink any water as we can sometime confuse detration with hunger. According to Are you hungry or thirsty? It's hard to tell “Misdiagnosing the sensation of thirst can easily mislead the body into thinking it needs food when what it’s really asking for is water. Moreover, the fact that the symptoms of dehydration... mimic those of hunger, contributes to people’s confusion between the two signals.” So make sure to drink some water if you're feeling a little bit hungry. However, if you have been drinking a reasonable amount of water then now it comes to the snacks themselves. If you’re looking for foods high in fiber you can eat gernal fruits, nuts. whole grain breads or crackers and cheese works as they contain carbohydrates. Protein powders or protein bars also work.

Types of Food in the Go

When it comes to have these foods on the go is still relatively easy as well. When it comes to cheese and nuts they are the easiest to have with on the go as they can be sold at most convenience stores. As for whole grains and crackers although you don’t need to be carrying around toast you can have a bag of pretzels with you. You just have to make sure that the ingredients are whole grains. According to 25 Nutritious (and Tasty) Healthy Snacks other snacks that you can use are, “Almonds, Cereal, Energy Bar, Popcorn and Dark Chocolate” Of course in portions.

With some trial and error you can find which snack will be most beifall to you as you hustle throughout the day. As long as you first take into consideration of the snacks proportions, which kind you would like, and if you are hitetatwd or not then you should be good.
